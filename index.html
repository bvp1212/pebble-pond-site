Pebble Pond — Privacy Policy

Effective July 10, 2026

Pebble Pond is a one-touch puzzle game. We designed it to know as little about you as possible: it has no accounts, no sign-in, no chat, and it never asks for your name, email, contacts, location, photos, or microphone.

What stays on your device

Your game progress — the levels you've reached, your stars, daily streak, and sound preference — is stored only on your device (local storage). It is never uploaded. Deleting the app deletes it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

When you choose to watch a rewarded ad, the ad is served by Google AdMob. To deliver and measure ads, Google may collect device information such as your device model, IP address, and advertising identifier, as described in Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob's data practices. Ads only play when you tap the ad option — the game never shows interruptive ads.

In-app purchases (RevenueCat, App Store / Google Play)

Purchases are processed by Apple or Google; we never see your payment details. We use RevenueCat to validate purchases, which receives a pseudonymous app-generated identifier and purchase receipt data, as described in RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Gameplay analytics (Google Firebase)

To understand how the game is played — for example which levels are most difficult — Pebble Pond sends anonymous gameplay events (such as "level 12 completed in 4 taps") to Google Firebase Analytics, along with standard app-usage information such as device model, country, and session length, as described in Firebase's privacy documentation. These events contain no names, contacts, or content you create. They are used solely to improve the game.

Children

Pebble Pond does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ad serving is configured for a general audience through the platforms above.

Your choices

  • You can reset your advertising identifier or limit ad tracking in your device settings (iOS: Settings › Privacy › Tracking / Apple Advertising; Android: Settings › Google › Ads).
  • You can play the entire game without ever watching an ad or making a purchase.
  • Deleting the app removes all locally stored progress.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the new version will be posted at this address with an updated date. For questions, reach the developer through Pebble Pond's App Store or Google Play listing — both include a contact option.
